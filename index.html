Welcome

We're a family owned and operated water purification and ice cream shop. Come try all of our purified waters: RO, re-mineralized, and Alkaline. We serve cold Thrifty Ice Cream and use it to make our delicious milkshakes. We also offer smoothies, snow cones, Indian Ice Golas, ice bags made with our amazing water and more!

Covid Update

As an essential business, we are still open and serving the Tucson community. We are now delivering through GrubHub, UberEATS, DoorDash, and Postmates to reduce the risk of overcrowding in our store. But all are welcome to come try our water and buy a sweet treat in person.
